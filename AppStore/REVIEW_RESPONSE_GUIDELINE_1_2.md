# Guideline 1.2 Response

Hello App Review Team,

Thank you for the review. We updated the app to meet the user-generated content safety requirements.

The new build includes:

- A Terms of Use agreement shown before registration or login. The terms state that objectionable content and abusive users are not tolerated.
- A content filter that blocks objectionable text before a letter can be posted.
- A report button on each letter detail screen. Reports are saved to our moderation queue and the reported letter is removed from the reporter's feed immediately.
- A block button on each letter detail screen. Blocking a user also creates a developer moderation report and removes that user's letters from the user's feed immediately.
- We review moderation reports within 24 hours. We remove objectionable content and suspend users who posted offending content.

The requested screen recording is attached in the App Review Information notes. It demonstrates:

1. The Terms of Use agreement before login.
2. The objectionable content reporting flow.
3. The abusive user blocking flow.

Thank you.
